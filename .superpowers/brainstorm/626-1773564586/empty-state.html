Give your story structure

Acts divide your story into meaningful phases — a beginning that hooks, a middle that builds tension, and an ending that resolves. Choose a structure that fits your story and we'll help you map it out.

Design details

  • Header bar stays the same (Timeline/List tabs, storyline filter, point count)
  • Sidebar stays normal — shows chapters grouped by storyline, just like the editor
  • Icon is a subtle illustration representing the plot grid with colored dots
  • Heading uses EB Garamond (the app's serif font) for warmth
  • Template cards have colored bars at top representing the relative act proportions
  • Beat counts shown per template so authors know what they're getting
  • Clicking a card opens the wizard modal (next section)
